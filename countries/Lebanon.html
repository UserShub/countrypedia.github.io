LEBANON

Lebanon offers plenty: from ancient Roman ruins, to well-preserved castles, limestone caves, historic Churches and Mosques, beautiful beaches nestled in the Mediterranean Sea, world-renowned Lebanese cuisine, nonstop nightlife and discothèques, to mountainous ski resorts.


Capital : Beirut


Majority Languages spoken : Arabic


Currency: Lebanese pound


Area: 10,230 km²


Major Cities : Beirut, Baalbek, Sidon


Geography : Lebanon is a small country in the Middle East, located at approximately 34˚N, 35˚E. It stretches along the eastern shore of the Mediterranean Sea and its length is almost three times its width.
