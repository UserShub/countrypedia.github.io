CHINA

China, officially the People's Republic of China, is a country in East Asia. It is the world's most populous country, with a population of more than 1.4 billion. China spans five geographical time zones and borders 14 different countries, the second most of any country in the world after Russia.


Capital : Beijing


Majority Languages spoken : Mandarin


Currency: Renminbi(formely Yuan)


Area: 9,388,211 km²


Major Cities : Beijing, Shanghai, Chengdu


Geography : China is a country located in East Asia with an area of 9,596,960 km². The exact land area can sometimes be challenged by border disputes, including those concerning Taiwan, Aksai Chin, the Trans-Karakoram Tract, the South China Sea Islands, the Senkaku Islands, and South Tibet.
