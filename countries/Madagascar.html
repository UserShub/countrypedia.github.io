Madagascar

Madagascar, officially the Republic of Madagascar, and previously known as the Malagasy Republic, is an island country in the Indian Ocean, approximately 400 kilometres off the coast of East Africa across the Mozambique Channel.


Capital : Antananarivo


Majority Languages spoken : Malagasy, French


Currency: Malagasy ariary


Area: 581,795 km²


Major Cities : Antananarivo, Mahajanga, Toliara


Geography : Madagascar consists of three parallel longitudinal zones—the central plateau, the coastal strip in the east, and the zone of low plateaus and plains in the west.
