Micronesia

The Federated States of Micronesia is a country spread across the western Pacific Ocean comprising more than 600 islands. Micronesia is made up of 4 island states: Pohnpei, Kosrae, Chuuk and Yap. The country is known for palm-shaded beaches, wreck-filled dives and ancient ruins, including Nan Madol, sunken basalt temples and burial vaults that extend out of a lagoon on Pohnpei.


Capital : Palikir


Majority Languages spoken : English


Currency: United States Dollar


Area: 700 km²


Major Cities : Palikir, Pohnpei, Chuuk Lagoon


Geography : Geography of the Federated States of Micronesia, a country located in the western Pacific Ocean, and in the Micronesia cultural and ecological sub-region of Oceania. While its total land area is very small at 702 km², it has the 14th largest exclusive economic zone at 2,996,419 km².
