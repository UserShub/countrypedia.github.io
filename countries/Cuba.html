CUBA

Cuba, officially the Republic of Cuba, is a country comprising the island of Cuba, as well as Isla de la Juventud and several minor archipelagos. Cuba is located where the northern Caribbean Sea, Gulf of Mexico, and Atlantic Ocean meet.


Capital : Havana


Majority Languages spoken : Spanish


Currency: Cuban peso


Area: 106,440 km²


Major Cities : Havana, Malecon, Vilanes


Geography : Cuba is an island nation in the Caribbean Sea. Its area is 110,860 km² including coastal and territorial waters with a land area of 109,884 km². This makes it the 8th largest island country in the world.
