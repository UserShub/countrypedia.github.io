United Arab Emirates

The United Arab Emirates or the Emirates, is a country in Western Asia. It is located at the eastern end of the Arabian Peninsula, and shares borders with Oman and Saudi Arabia, while having maritime borders in the Persian Gulf with Qatar and Iran.

Geography : The United Arab Emirates (UAE) is a federation of seven different emirates located in the Middle East, on the Arabian Peninsula. The constituent emirates are: Abu Dhabi, Ajman, Dubai, Fujairah, Ras al-Khaimah, Sharjah and Umm al-Quwain. The UAE is bordered by Oman and Saudi Arabia as well as the Gulf of Oman and the Persian Gulf. The exact size of the UAE is unknown because of several disputed claims to several islands in the Persian Gulf. Also, many of its land borders remain un-demarcated.
