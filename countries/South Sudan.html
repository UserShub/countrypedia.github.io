South Sudan

South Sudan, officially known as the Republic of South Sudan, is a landlocked country in east/central Africa. It is landlocked by Ethiopia, Sudan, Central African Republic, Democratic Republic of the Congo, Uganda and Kenya.

Geography : The terrain of South Sudan features massive plains and plateaus. Two distinct highland areas exist in the country.
