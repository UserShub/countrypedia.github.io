LIBYA

Libya, officially the State of Libya, is a country in the Maghreb region in North Africa. It is bordered by the Mediterranean Sea to the north, Egypt to the east, Sudan to the southeast, Chad to the south, Niger to the southwest, Algeria to the west, and Tunisia to the northwest.


Capital : Tripoli


Majority Languages spoken : Arabic


Currency: Libyan dinar


Area: 1,759,540 kmÂ²


Major Cities : Tripoli, Leptis Magna, Gadamis


Geography : Libya is almost entirely covered by the Libyan Desert, a flat plateau that's part of the Sahara, the world's largest hot desert. Libya is so dry that no permanent rivers flow through its boundaries. Water may flow beneath the ground and occasionally seep aboveground into dry streambeds called wadis.
